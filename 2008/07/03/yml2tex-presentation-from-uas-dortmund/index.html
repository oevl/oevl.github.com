yml2tex presentation from UAS Dortmund

Published 3rd July 2008

I had the chance to give a small talk at the UAS Dortmund and introduce the yml2tex Python script to generate LaTeX Beamer presentations out of YAML files.

Although the presentation is in German, I think it might help to better understand the syntax by seeing a practical example.

The whole presentation with all the files needed to generate it can be downloaded in a ZIP file.
